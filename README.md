## Visual Identity
- **Accent**: Deep forest green (hue 150, sat 50) — PNW evergreen, earthy craft
- **Secondary**: Warm amber (#d97706) — matching their white-yellow logo palette
- **Display Font**: DM Serif Display | **Body Font**: Source Sans 3
- **Tagline**: "Endless Passion. Accessible Brilliance."
- **Loyalty Currency**: Block Points

## Beer List (12 beers from Untappd/BeerAdvocate/TapHunter/block15.com)
- 4 flagships: Sticky Hands (8.1% IIPA, 28K+ Untappd ratings, 110 IBU), Gloria! (Pilsner), Nebula Stout, The Crew (Light Lager)
- 4 year-round: Fluffhead (Hazy IPA), Step Outside Pale Ale, Hop Circus, Gary's Imperial WC IPA
- 4 limited/barrel: Super Nebula Coffee (Imperial Stout w/ Bespoken Coffee), Kriek (wild-fermented Oud Bruin w/ cherries), Figgy Pudding (BA Old Ale), Cassidy (wild farmhouse w/ spring flowers + Sauternes barrels)

## Key Brand Details
- Founded 2008 by Nick and Kristen Arzner, brewer-owned from day one
- 3 Corvallis locations: Downtown Pub (300 SW Jefferson), Southtown Tap Room (3415 SW Deschutes), Caves Restaurant
- Annual Yakima hop selection trips — blind evaluation process
- Deep wild/sour program with temperature-controlled barrel room
- Locally sourced PNW cuisine — Painted Hills Beef, Carlton Farms pork
- Existing Mug Club program — loyalty app complements this
- Distribution through Block 15 Distribution across PNW
